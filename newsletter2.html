Why North Americans Choose Panama For A Second Home

Panama has become an increasingly attractive destination for North Americans seeking a second residency. This trend is driven by several factors, including Panama's stable and growing economy, favorable tax policies, and high quality of life. The country boasts modern infrastructure, world-class healthcare, and a lower cost of living compared to many North American cities. Additionally, Panama offers various legal pathways for residency, such as the Friendly Nations Visa and Pensionado Program, making it an appealing choice for investors, retirees, and remote workers. Whether looking to diversify investments, enjoy a tropical lifestyle, or secure a retirement haven, many North Americans are finding Panama to be the ideal destination.
